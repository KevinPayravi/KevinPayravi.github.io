About Me

Hi - I'm Kevin, a college student who grew up and lives in the lovely Northeast Ohio (except when I'm away at college).

I'm currently a second-year college student studying Computer Science & Engineering at The Ohio State University in Columbus. I love working in development (both software and web), along with graphic design, and also enjoy working with social media management and marketing on the side. I've done most of my work so far with object-oriented programming languages (including Java, C++, and C#). For web development, I have experience with HTML and CSS, and am currently teaching myself Javascript.

Other various works in my free time include editing a variety of wikis (namely Wikipedia) and some amateur photography (which I upload under free licenses to the Wikimedia Commons).
